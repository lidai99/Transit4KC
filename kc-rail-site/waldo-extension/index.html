The Time Is Right To Reconnect Brookside -

Read slide text
THE TIME IS RIGHT TO RECONNECT BROOKSIDE -
WALDO
• Rail Is Back: Streetcars returned to Kansas City in 2016 with ridership that has exceeded
all expectations. Extensions to the UMKC and the Riverfront are set to open in 2025 and
2026 respectively.
• Community: Seeing the success of the Streetcar Starter Line, communities across the
region are having conversations about bringing rail transit to their neighborhoods.
• Right Fit: The Brookside – Waldo area was designed around rail transit and much of the
civil infrastructure remains in place (bridges, earthwork, and grading) on the Country Club
Right of Way (“CCROW”)
• Path Forward: With the UMKC extension bringing rail to the northern end of the CCROW,
there is now an ability to extend the streetcar to Brookside / Waldo quickly and at a lower
cost than other extensions.

Sources: Next Rail KC Study, Public News Sources

FINANCIAL PLAN – TDD

• The aforementioned new Waldo Extension TDD will
be structured similarly to the existing MSE TDD:
• 1% sales tax within ~1/3 mile of the extension
• Special assessment on property within ~1/3 mile of
the route
• The original Downtown TDD special assessment
zone is set to expire in 2037
• An extended Downtown special assessment zone
could ensure longterm funding for existing Streetcar
while capturing additional funding for new
extensions
